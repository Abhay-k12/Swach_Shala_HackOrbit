What is Swachh Shala?

Swachh Shala is a hygiene monitoring platform where government authorities can send inspection requests to institutions. Schools upload area photos (washrooms, kitchens, etc.), and our AI model generates a hygiene report and score. The system ensures transparency, cleanliness, and smart decision-making for funding and policies.

Real-Time Inspections

Authorities can issue timed inspection notices to schools and institutions.

AI-Based Hygiene Detection

Photos are scanned by AI models that assign cleanliness scores and generate reports.

Smart Reporting

Reports are shared with schools and authorities for actions and evaluations.
